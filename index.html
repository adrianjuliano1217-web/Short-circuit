Simulation Guide

Yellow arrows show current flow.
Speed depends on current.
Zero current means no flow.

Series
One path only.
If one resistor breaks, everything stops.

Parallel
Three separate branches.
Current splits at the intersection.
One broken branch does not stop the others.

Fan
Speed depends on total current.
Click wire or fan
